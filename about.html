Our Story

At Kansas Correctional Industries (KCI), our story is one of transformation, opportunity, and community impact. Established as a program within the Kansas Department of Corrections, KCI's mission has always been to provide incarcerated individuals with valuable work experience, helping them gain the skills they need to successfully reenter society.

What started as a small initiative has grown into a self-sustaining operation offering high-quality products and services to state agencies, non-profits, schools, and other eligible organizations. From furniture and textiles to metal fabrication and printing, our diverse range of products is crafted with care and purpose, all while creating opportunities for personal growth and rehabilitation.

Through KCI, residents are not just learning a trade; they are rebuilding their lives, gaining confidence, and contributing to their communities.

We're proud to be part of a program that not only produces exceptional products but also empowers individuals, strengthens the workforce, and makes a lasting difference in the lives of those we serve. Our story is about growth, opportunity, and the power of industry to change lives for the better.

Who We Benefit

Kansas Correctional Industries benefits a wide range of individuals and organizations, including incarcerated residents gaining valuable work experience, state agencies, non-profits, schools, and local businesses receiving high-quality, cost-effective products. By supporting KCI, you contribute to a stronger, safer community while helping individuals build a foundation for successful reentry.

Private Companies

Companies receive exceptional craftsmanship while supporting a program that builds a skilled workforce for the future.

KDOC Residents

Through hands-on training in real-world industries, residents gain valuable skills, work experience, and a sense of purpose.

KDOC Facilities

KCI programs provide structured, meaningful work that benefits correctional facilities by promoting responsibility, discipline, and rehabilitation.

Local & State Agencies

KCI reduces taxpayer burdens by operating as a self-sustaining entity while lowering recidivism through workforce development.
