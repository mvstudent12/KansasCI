Impact Through Industry

Kansas Correctional Industries creates a lasting impact on businesses, individuals, and communities by providing high-quality products while fostering rehabilitation and economic growth.

For Companies: High-Quality, Cost-Effective Solutions

KCI partners with businesses and organizations to deliver reliable, competitively priced products and services. By choosing KCI, companies receive exceptional craftsmanship while supporting a program that builds a skilled workforce for the future.

For Residents: Job Training & Second Chances

Through hands-on training in real-world industries, residents gain valuable skills, work experience, and a sense of purpose. These opportunities help individuals successfully reintegrate into society with a stronger foundation for employment and personal growth.

For the State: Economic & Social Benefits

KCI reduces taxpayer burdens by operating as a self-sustaining entity while lowering recidivism through workforce development. The program prepares individuals for post-incarceration employment, ultimately contributing to a stronger economy and safer communities.

For Facilities: Productive & Engaged Populations

KCI programs provide structured, meaningful work that benefits correctional facilities by promoting responsibility, discipline, and rehabilitation. These efforts contribute to a safer, more positive environment within the facilities.

Turning Skills Into Success

KCI is dedicated to providing high-quality products and services while fostering skill development and rehabilitation for incarcerated individuals.

  • As a self-sustaining program within the Kansas Department of Corrections, KCI offers real-world job training in industries such as manufacturing, textiles, printing, and more.
  • By equipping residents with valuable skills and work experience, KCI helps reduce recidivism and prepares individuals for successful reintegration into the workforce. At the same time, our customers—state agencies, non-profits, and government organizations—benefit from competitively priced, high-quality products designed to meet their unique needs.
  • Through our commitment to excellence, KCI creates lasting impacts on individuals, businesses, and communities across Kansas.

Industries with a Mission

At Kansas Correctional Industries, our mission is to provide quality products while creating opportunities for skill development and successful reentry. Through our work, we strive to make a lasting impact on individuals, businesses, and communities.

  • Empowering Individuals - We equip residents with hands-on training and real-world job skills to support successful reintegration.
  • Delivering Quality - Our products and services meet the highest standards, ensuring reliability and value for our customers.
  • Strengthening Communities - By reducing recidivism and promoting economic growth, we contribute to a safer and more productive Kansas.

Building Skills, Changing Lives

  • A Future of Empowerment - To be a leader in providing transformative work opportunities that foster personal growth and career readiness.
  • Innovative Partnerships - To build strong, lasting collaborations with businesses, government agencies, and organizations to create sustainable social impact.
  • Excellence in Craftsmanship - To continually set the standard for quality, integrity, and innovation in the products and services we offer.

Crafting a Better Future

Kansas Correctional Industries has two objectives to enhance the work training programs we currently run statewide.

  • The first is to ensure every qualified CI resident who wants assistance is "work ready" and employable upon release.
  • The second objective is to link with community partners on job readiness, job placement, and Vocational Education to provide a pathway of continuous employment from prison into the community.
